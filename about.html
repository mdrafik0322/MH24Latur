ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2024, MH24 began with a simple passion for exceptional coffee. What started as a small café in the heart of the city has now grown into a beloved community hub where people gather to enjoy premium coffee and meaningful connections.

Our journey began when our founder, Rafik Pathan, discovered his love for coffee during his travels across coffee-growing regions of the world. Inspired by the craftsmanship and dedication of coffee farmers, he envisioned a place where this artistry could be celebrated and shared.

Today, MH24 is more than just a coffee shop – it's a destination for coffee enthusiasts, remote workers, and anyone seeking a warm, welcoming environment to enjoy life's simple pleasures.

Our Mission

At MH24, our mission is to create extraordinary coffee experiences that bring people together. We are committed to sourcing the finest beans from sustainable farms, crafting each cup with precision and care, and fostering a community where every visitor feels at home.

We believe that great coffee has the power to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. Through our dedication to quality, sustainability, and community, we strive to make MH24 a place where people can connect, create, and celebrate life's beautiful moments.
